Pic template studio Privacy Policy

Last updated: __PRIVACY_POLICY_UPDATED__

Summary

Pic template studio is designed to edit photos on your device. The app does not provide an account system or a developer-operated cloud photo-upload service.

Photos and camera

The app accesses a photo only after you choose it from the system picker or choose Camera. Selected photos are used to show the editor, create cutouts, apply adjustments, and export the image. Camera access is used only when you start the Camera action.

Local storage

Saved projects, templates, temporary previews, and model files are stored locally on your device. You can remove saved projects and templates in the app. Uninstalling the app or clearing its storage removes app-private data. Images that you intentionally save to Gallery or Downloads remain in those public folders until you delete them.

Sharing and exports

The app saves to Gallery or Downloads only when you choose an export destination. When you choose Share, Android opens the system share sheet and you choose the receiving app or service. The privacy practices of that destination apply after you send a file.

Collection and sale of data

This release is configured without advertising, analytics, accounts, or a developer-operated photo-upload service. The developer does not sell selected photos or project data.

Children

This app is a general photo-editing utility. It does not include social features, public profiles, messaging, or targeted ads.

Changes to this policy

Material changes will be reflected on this page and in a future app update when appropriate.
